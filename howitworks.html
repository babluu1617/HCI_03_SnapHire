Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for the photography session?

Payment is handled securely through our platform. You'll only be charged after confirming your booking, and the payment is held in escrow until the session is completed.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

Cancellations made 48 hours before the scheduled session are eligible for a full refund. Please check individual photographer's cancellation policies for specific terms.

How do I receive my photos?

Your photographer will upload the edited photos to your private gallery on SnapHire, where you can download them directly or share them with friends and family.
